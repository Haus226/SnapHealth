Induction therapy (First Phase) : Kill most of the leukemia cells in the blood and bone marrow and to restore normal blood cell production.
Consolidation therapy (Second Phase) : Aimed at destroying any remaining leukemia in the body.
Maintenance therapy (Third Phase) : Prevent leukemia cells from regrowing.
